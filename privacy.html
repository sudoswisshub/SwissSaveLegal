Privacy and Data Handling

Privacy Policy

This page explains what SwissSave stores, how subscription billing is handled through Apple, and what control users have over their own data.

Last updated: March 31, 2026 Local-first by design

Information You Provide

SwissSave may store the financial information, notes, budgets, and subscription details you enter in order to provide the app's features.

How Data Is Stored

SwissSave is designed to keep your data on your device unless you explicitly choose to export, share, or sync it using app functionality or Apple platform services.

Data Sharing

SwissSave does not sell your personal data. Information is not shared with third parties except when necessary to provide Apple-managed functionality or when you explicitly initiate an action such as exporting or sharing content.

Analytics and Diagnostics

SwissSave does not include third-party advertising SDKs. If analytics, diagnostics, or crash reporting are introduced in the future, this policy will be updated.

Third-Party Services

SwissSave may rely on Apple frameworks and Apple platform services. Those services are governed by Apple's own terms and privacy practices.

Subscriptions

SwissSave Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive your full payment card or billing details.

Billing, renewals, cancellations, and subscription management are handled through your Apple Account.

Your Choices

You can delete app data by removing the app or using any in-app reset or deletion features that may be provided.

Children's Privacy

SwissSave is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate authorization.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
