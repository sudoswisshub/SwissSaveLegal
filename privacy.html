Privacy Policy – SwissSave

Last updated: November 2025

SwissSave respects your privacy and is designed to store as much data as possible locally on your device.

1. Data We Collect

SwissSave stores your inputs, financial notes, and calculation data only on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

2. Analytics

SwissSave does not track analytics unless explicitly enabled by you.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use Apple frameworks (for example, VisionKit). These services follow Apple's privacy standards.

4. Your Rights

You may delete your data at any time by removing the app or using the in-app reset function.
